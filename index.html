History of Reggae

Reggae emerged in the late 1960s and quickly became a voice for the oppressed. Influenced by ska and rocksteady, reggae music promotes peace, love, and social justice. Bob Marley remains one of the most influential figures in reggae.

Core Elements of Reggae

  1. Sound
    • Offbeat guitar chops
    • Heavy basslines
    • Laid-back drum rhythms
  2. Lyrics
    • Messages of love and resistance
    • Spiritual and cultural themes
